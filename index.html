Welcome to Snow Pine Huskies

Welcome to Snow Pine Huskies. We are a family-run business that breeds and sells Siberian Husky puppies. Our Siberian Huskies are AKC registered. We are located in Illinois in the Chicagoland area.

If you want to see what puppies are currently for sale, check out "Current Litters". In "Past Litters" you can see the puppies we have raised over the years. All our parent dogs are listed under "Parents".

Have questions or want to buy a puppy? Go to "Contact Us" to ask us your questions or to let us know which puppy you are interested in purchasing.
